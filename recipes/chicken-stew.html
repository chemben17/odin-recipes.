Chicken Stew

Ingedients

  • 2 Ibs chicken thighs
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 habanero pepper use 1/2 if you don't want it too spicy
  • 1/2 cup Olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken stock (broth)
  • 1 onions
  • 1 teaspoon bouillon powder
  • thyme in desired quantity
  • Black pepper
  • Curry Powder
  • Curry powder
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and pat dry the chicken
  2. Rub the chicken thigh with salt and black pepper, and leave them in a bowl
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan
  4. Brown the Chicken of each side (about 10mins each)
  5. Cut the onions into two and chop one part into small pieces
  6. When the chicken is done. take it out and set it aside
  7. Blend the tomatoes, red bell pepper, habanero pepper, and the other half of the onion.
  8. Saute the choppped onion in the oil till translucent
  9. Pour the blended tomato and pepper mix and boil for 5mins
  10. Add chicken, stock, bouillon, curry and thyme
  11. Taste for salt and add more if necessary
  12. Cook for 20mins or until chicken is done
